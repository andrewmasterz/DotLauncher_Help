Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Overview

Dot Launcher is a minimalist Android home screen launcher. We are committed to your privacy. This policy explains what data (if any) is collected and how it is used.

Data We Collect

Dot Launcher does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All preferences — including your grid layout, display mode, colour settings, and Pro access state — are stored locally on your device only and never leave it.

In-App Purchases

Dot Launcher Pro is available as a one-time in-app purchase. All payment processing is handled entirely by Google Play. We do not receive, store, or process any payment card or billing details.

Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for information on how your payment data is handled by Google.

Permissions

Dot Launcher requests the following permissions:

  • QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES — Required to display the list of installed apps on your home screen.
  • REQUEST_DELETE_PACKAGES — Required to allow you to uninstall apps from within the launcher.
  • REQUEST_IGNORE_BATTERY_OPTIMIZATIONS — Requested so the launcher remains available immediately when you press the Home button, even after periods of inactivity.

No permission is used for data collection, advertising, or analytics.

Third-Party Services

Dot Launcher does not use analytics platforms, advertising networks, crash reporting SDKs, or any other third-party tracking services. The only third-party component is Google Play Billing (used for in-app purchases), which is governed by Google's own privacy policy.

Children's Privacy

Dot Launcher does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Because we collect no personal data at all, the app is safe for general use.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
